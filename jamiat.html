Key Strategic Insights
🎯 Wasooli Excellence
Murabbi Training achieved exceptional 87% wasooli rate (13/15), demonstrating the power of intensive, specialized programs for committed participants.
📈 Scale vs Quality
While Prophetic Wisdom Batch 1 reached massive scale (1,120 registrations), the wasooli rate was lower (28%), suggesting optimization opportunities for large-scale programs.
🏆 Retention Success
100% wasooli-to-member conversion in specialized courses (Murabbis: 14/14, Study Circle: 20/20) shows the effectiveness of targeted content delivery.
🎓 Content Impact
IDS, Seerat, and Islahi content shows strongest retention rates, while traditional Tarbiyah content may need refreshed delivery methods.
📊 Participation Patterns
Average 23% participation rate suggests need for better pre-course engagement and commitment mechanisms to reduce drop-offs.
🔮 Future Strategy
Focus on mid-sized courses (30-60 registrations) with specialized content for optimal balance of reach and effectiveness.
